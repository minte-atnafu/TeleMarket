About Telemarket Ethiopia

Telemarket Ethiopia is an innovative final-year project developed by Samuel Gashu and Mintesinot Atnafu, Computer Engineering students at Addis Ababa University, under the guidance of Dr. Biniam T. Our vision is to revolutionize the Ethiopian e-commerce landscape by introducing a smart, centralized platform driven by Artificial Intelligence.

Born out of a deep understanding of the fragmented and inefficient online shopping experience in Ethiopia, Telemarket Ethiopia aims to unify product discovery, customer insights, and seller accessibility through cutting-edge machine learning models and intelligent system design. Our goal is to empower both consumers and merchants with a seamless, data-driven digital marketplace tailored to local needs.

With a clear mission to enhance e-commerce through technology, we are committed to building scalable, secure, and ethically responsible solutions that respect user data and address the unique challenges of our economy.
